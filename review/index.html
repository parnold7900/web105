UFC 298

UFC 298 took place in San Francisco on January 17, 2024. The co-main event was Robert Whittaker and Paulo Costa. And for the featherweight championship Ilia Topuria versus Alexander Volkanovski. The co-main event was a great fight with Robert Whittaker winning by decision. But the main event was the best fight of the night.

Alexander Volkanovski vs. Ilia Topuria

Alexander Volkanovski and Ilia Topuria stepping in the ring Saturday for the Feather Weight World Champshionship. The fight was scheduled for five, 5-minute rounds. The fight was stopped when Ilia Topuria knocked out Alexander Volkanovski.

The knockout

It seemed as though Alexander Volkanovski was starting to find his rhythm. His pace was increasing, and Ilia Topuria was showing some signs of damage. The two got into and exchange which resulted in Alexander Volkanovski having his back against the fence. Ilia Topuria connected with a combo followed by a devastating right hand that knocked Alexander Volkanovski out.
